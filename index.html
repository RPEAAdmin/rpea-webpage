Background

Agriculture serves as the primary pillar of Myanmar’s economy, with approximately 75% of the total population residing in rural areas. To support the development of these rural regions and enhance the capacity of their communities, the Rural People's Empowerment Association (RPEA) was established. The association is comprised of members with extensive experience in the development sector, all of whom are dedicated to actively participating in rural development initiatives nationwide. Guided by these objectives, RPEA was founded on February 12, 2024.

Official Registration: Officially granted its registration certificate by the Union Registration Board of the Ministry of Home Affairs on November 8, 2024, under Registration Number 1/Internal/0080(2022). This registration authorizes the organization to conduct development operations across all States and Regions within the country.

Strategic Objectives

In collaboration with the Ministry of Co-operatives and Rural Development:

  • To implement and expand capacity-building programs that enhance the sustainable livelihoods of local community members.
  • To actively participate in national poverty reduction initiatives and foster the development of resilient communities.
  • To facilitate and support the improvement of production capacities for impoverished individuals and stakeholders.

Core Values

  • ⚖️ High responsibility, accountability, operational transparency, and impartiality.
  • 🤝 Mutual respect among members and promoting organizational unity.
  • 👩‍👦 Upholding the dignity of women and children; gender-sensitive practices.
  • 🏡 Honoring local regulations, traditions, and community standards.
  • 🌍 Respecting individual differences and diverse perspectives.
  • 🇲🇲 Strictly adhering to the laws, rules, and regulations mandated by the State.

Core Operational Activities

Aid & Resources

Providing technical assistance and financial aid for poverty alleviation initiatives.

Infrastructure

Developing farm roads, small bridges, latrines, and clean drinking water facilities.

WASH Services

Implementing rural water supply, environmental sanitation, and hygiene services.

Vocational Training

Tailoring classes, engine repair courses, and sustainable agricultural practices.
